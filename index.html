Who

For the first time in history, we the people can collaborate on the exploration, drive, and action of exploring the cosmos. Space, the final frontier.

This project, with the help of some of the most ambitious leaders in our time, will collaborate with we the people to build a spaceport.

It's mission...


To expand human interests here on earth, and beyond!

What

Space Ranch is the next evolution in governance and space.

A quick to market, multi phase space port focusing on putting critical research and space infrastructure into orbit first, then expanding to human lead missions in the future.

Surrounded by some of the most beautiful land in Wyoming, as the project expands we will host a research campus, incubator, and nature reserve on the property.

Where

The 4,000 Acre Cole Ranch, located just north of Sundance Wyoming, is nestled in between two sets of mountains and is shielded from some of the prevailing winds and driving snows that the lower portions of the state are notorious for.

Why

Wyoming provides the open business infrustructure to create unique models for revolutionary proof cases to be built.

The possibility of using a DAO for the administration of Space Ranch opens ownership potential to all Wyoming Residents as well as the world at large.

The shift in perspective of space being something that one entity owns to something that everyone owns is how we embolden our youth to build a future that we all have a stake in.
